What Tint Calendar does

Tint Calendar is a lightweight browser extension that lets you customize event colors in your calendar more easily.

You can select, change, add, and remove colors using a color picker, and your chosen colors are stored locally in your browser.

No accounts, no complicated setup, no unnecessary clutter. Just better control over how your calendar looks.

Features

Custom event colors

Apply your own colors to calendar events and make your schedule easier to scan.

Persistent preferences

Your selected colors are saved locally, so they stay after page refresh.

Simple color management

Add and remove colors directly through an integrated color picker.

How it works

1. Open your calendar.

2. Right click on event -> pick a color for an event.

3. Add, or remove colors as needed.

4. Your preferences remain stored locally in your browser.

5. You can also change a color of event text.
