How it works

Up and running in seconds

1
Open your Google Calendar in Chrome.
2
Right-click any event and pick a color from the palette.
3
Add or remove custom colors using the built-in color picker.
4
Your preferences are saved locally — they persist across every visit.
5
Optionally customize the text color of any event for extra clarity.
