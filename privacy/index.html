Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Tint Calendar is a lightweight Chrome extension that helps you customize calendar event colors. Privacy is built in by design — there are no accounts, no servers, and nothing leaves your device.

Information Collection

Tint Calendar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. We have no access to anything on your device.

Local Storage

The extension stores your color preferences locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device and is used solely to persist your customizations across page reloads and sessions.

Third-Party Services

Tint Calendar does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or any third-party data collection tools of any kind.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, transfer, rent, or share your data with any third parties — because we don't have any of your data to begin with.

Changes to This Policy

If future versions of Tint Calendar introduce features that affect data handling, this policy will be updated and the date above will reflect the change.
