Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Tint Calendar is designed to help users customize calendar event colors with a simple and lightweight browser extension.

Information Collection

Tint Calendar does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.

Local Storage

The extension stores color preferences locally in your browser so your customizations can persist across page reloads and future visits.

Third-Party Services

Tint Calendar does not use advertising trackers, analytics services, or third-party data collection tools.

Data Sharing

Tint Calendar does not sell, transfer, or share your data with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

If Tint Calendar introduces new functionality that affects data usage in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
